# Privacy Policy

Gordon is designed to be privacy-first.

Data is stored locally on the device by default. Gordon does not require an account and does not upload personal data unless the user explicitly exports or shares a report.

Optional integrations (such as HealthKit or Health Connect) are opt-in.

Users control what information is included in exported reports.
